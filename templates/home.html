Core Solutions

We manage your clinical trial payments and provide reconciliation, budgeting, and financial analysis services

Site Payment Management

Our team of experts simplifies the payment process for your clinical trial. By comparing the costs in your clinical trial agreement with the visit and procedure data in your EDC (Electronic Data Capture) system, we accurately calculate the payments owed to sites, investigators, patients, and all other service providers, including pharmacies and labs.

Reconciliation

We ensure financial accuracy and foresight for your clinical trial. Our process reconciles costs in clinical trial agreements and amendments against payment records, forecasts future payments based on key study milestones like closure and database lock, and accurately analyzes all Ethics Committee payments stemming from protocol changes.

Budgeting

Our budgeting service helps you create and manage a robust financial plan for your clinical trial. We develop detailed budgets, forecast expenses, and closely track spending to prevent overruns, giving you complete financial control.

Financial Analysis

We analyze the financial performance of your clinical trial. This includes generating reports on spending trends, forecasting future costs, and identifying opportunities to optimize spending.
